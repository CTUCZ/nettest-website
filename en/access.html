Accessibility Statement

The Czech Telecommunication Office undertakes to make available the website of its measurement tool CTU-NetTest: https://nettest.cz/ and the CTU-NetTest mobile application for Android and iOS in accordance with Act No.99/2019 Coll., on Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications and the amendment to Act No.365/2000 Coll., on Public Administration Information Systems and on Amendments to Certain Other Acts, as amended (hereinafter referred to as the "Accessibility Act"), which implements Directive (EU) 2016/2102 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 26 October 2016 on the Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications of public sector entities.

This accessibility statement applies to the website of the publicly available CTU-NetTest measurement tool https://nettest.cz/ and the CTU-NetTest mobile application for Android and iOS.

Compliance status

This website is in full compliance with Act No. 99/2019 Coll., on Accessibility of Websites and Mobile Applications and on amending Act No. 365/2000 Coll., on Public Administration Information Systems and on the amendment of other acts.

Information presented in non-standard formats

The website also refers to information in the form of PDF and XLSX documents. In the case of the PDF format, these are mainly the Guide to Certified Measurement, which contains detailed instructions, including explanations of related concepts and procedures. Reference is also made to legally binding documents on the main website of the Czech Telecommunication Office (https://www.ctu.gov.cz) or to decrees on the official notice board. All documents are electronically signed and there is currently no other alternative.

A document containing the results of the certified measurements is also available in PDF format. This format has been chosen in view of the use of this document as a basis for exercising the right to complain or object to the handling of complaints about electronic communications services. This means primarily for printing or the possibility to electronically sign the file in the case of electronic submission.

The PDF and XLSX formats are used to export results and export open data due to the scale and size of the results. Alternatively, selected results can be exported also to CSV format or can be viewed and filtered directly on the website.

To view these documents, you can download free versions of open source office applications and PDF readers. Most web browsers can also open PDF documents.

Preparation of this accessibility statement

The Accessibility Statement was drawn up on 10 January, 2024.

The method of self-assessment carried out by the Czech Telecommunications Office was used to prepare this statement.

The statement was revised on 30 January, 2024.

Feedback and contact details

We are still working on fixing bugs and improving user-friendliness to make this site seamlessly accessible to all users. If you have any questions or comments about accessibility, please feel free to contact us at podatelna@ctu.cz.
